About the Game

Nanotech Warrior is a visually dynamic 2D platformer that explores humanity's complex relationship with artificial intelligence through the lens of classic gaming aesthetics. Set in the floating metropolis of Skyhaven, the game melds retro platforming mechanics with contemporary themes of technological advancement and environmental preservation. Nanotech Warrior was created in Unity with assets created in photoshop and credited found assets.

Key Features

  • Multiple levels with increasing difficulty
  • Interactive network defense mechanics
  • Educational cybersecurity concepts
  • Engaging storyline with character development
  • Stunning visuals and sound design

Technologies Used

Unity C# Photoshop Adobe After Effects
